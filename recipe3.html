The Pepperoni Pizza Wrap

From Spoon University

Total Time: 6 Minutes
Appliances needed: Microwave
User Rating: ✦ ✦ ✦ ✦ ✧
Health Rating: ♥ ♥ ♥ ♡ ♡

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 medium flour tortilla wrap
  • 2 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1 stick light string cheese
  • 6 slices pepperoni
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder

PREPARATION

    Place the wrap on a microwave-safe plate.

    Pull apart the string cheese into even pieces.

    Spread the pizza sauce over the tortilla, followed by the cheese and pepperoni slices. 4. Season the pizza as desired before wrapping it like a burrito.

    Microwave for 30 seconds while monitoring it.

    Let cool and enjoy!

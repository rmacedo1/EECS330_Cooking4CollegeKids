Pasta with Garbanzo Beans

Total Time: 10-15 Minutes
Appliances needed: Microwave, strainer, bowl
User Rating: ✦ ✦ ✦ ✦ ✧
Health Rating: ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♡

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta, any available
  • Vegetables
  • Parmesan cheese
  • garbanzo beans

    • Optional:
    • Sauce

PREPARATION

    Place a bowl on top of a plate and put it in the microwave just in case any water spills over. Take the suggested cooking time from the pasta box and add 3-4 minutes. Check on the pasta when it's done and sample a piece or two. If the pasta is at desired doneness, strain the pasta over the sink.

    While pasta is cooking, chop desired vegetables up into bite-sized pieces, as uniform as possible. When pasta is finished, place vegetables in a bowl, add water, and cover. Microwave for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables are desired tenderness.

    Strain and rinse garbanzo beans.

    Stir pasta, vegetables, and garbanzo beans together. Add cheese, and enjoy.

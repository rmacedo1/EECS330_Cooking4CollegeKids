The Traditional Vegetable Wrap

From Spoon University

Total Time: 5 Minutes
Appliances needed: None
User Rating: ✦ ✦ ✦ ✦ ✦
Health Rating: ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♡

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 medium spinach wrap
  • 2 tablespoons plain hummus
  • ¼ cup spinach
  • 2 tablespoons shredded carrots
  • ½ avocado
  • ¼ red pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper

PREPARATION

    Cut the avocado and red pepper into thin slices.

    Cover the spinach wrap with hummus, carrots, avocado, peppers and spinach.

    Season wrap with salt and pepper.

    Roll the wrap and enjoy cold.
